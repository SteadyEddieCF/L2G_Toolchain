# Known Limitations — v1.8.1

- Inherited applicability stores declared source metadata but does not synchronize parent content.
- Local supplements, overrides, stale-parent detection, and parent-change impact are not implemented.
- Approval values are tracking placeholders, not governed signatures or immutable approvals.
- Consolidated/module CRM and module Word Review are not implemented.
- Conflict detection, baselines, version comparison, and configurable rules are not implemented.
- No evidence binaries, multi-user backend, cloud discovery, or direct external integration is included.

These are deliberate roadmap boundaries, not hidden acceptance claims.
