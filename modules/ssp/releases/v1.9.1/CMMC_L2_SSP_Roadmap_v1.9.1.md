# SSP Roadmap after v1.9.1

1. **v1.9.2 — Evidence freshness and ownership reminders (suggested).** Local due-date and review-cycle support without telemetry or automatic compliance conclusions.
2. **v1.9.3 — Module dependency visualization and export (suggested).**
3. **v1.9.4 — Controlled bulk authoring and review operations (suggested).**
4. **v1.9.5 — Long-term compatibility and archival validation pack (suggested).**
5. **v1.9.6 — Archival signature and custody receipt adapters (suggested).** Optional adapters only; no built-in identity claim.
6. **v1.9.7 — Portfolio lifecycle and retirement workflow (suggested).**
7. **v1.9.8 — Cross-release portfolio migration rehearsal and recovery pack (suggested).**
8. **v1.9.9 — Pilot hardening and governed production-candidate closure (suggested).**
9. **v2.0.0 — Major-version governance review (suggested).** Only after pilot evidence and explicit authorization.
10. **Post-v2.0 — External integration adapters (suggested).** Separately authorized, offline-safe contracts only.

No post-v1.9.1 item is committed. All releases remain bounded, offline-first, independently versioned, and SSP-only.
