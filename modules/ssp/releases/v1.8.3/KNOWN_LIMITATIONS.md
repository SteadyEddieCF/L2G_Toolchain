# Known Limitations — v1.8.3

- Conflict escalation is authoring workflow, not formal approval or adjudication.
- Preserve/defer decisions do not alter the old child snapshot; a newer parent change reopens review.
- Consolidated/module CRM, module Word Review, formal approvals, immutable baselines, configurable rules, and multi-user synchronization remain deferred.
- Fingerprints are deterministic change detectors, not cryptographic signatures.
