# CMMC L2 SSP Modern Editable v1.8.6 — Release Notes

## Added
- Locally asserted, scope-aware Author, Reviewer, Approver, and Administrator role assignments.
- Portfolio or selected-module submission rounds bound to deterministic content fingerprints.
- Reviewer dispositions: Ready for approval, Changes requested, and Abstain.
- Formal authoring approval records with submitter/reviewer/approver segregation of duties.
- Approval staleness detection when governed content changes after approval.
- Revocation that preserves the original approval and records actor, time, and reason.
- Exportable review and approval register JSON.

## Boundary
This is an offline authoring-governance workflow. Locally entered identities are not authenticated accounts. Approval does not create an assessment finding, readiness determination, certification decision, compliance conclusion, or immutable baseline.
