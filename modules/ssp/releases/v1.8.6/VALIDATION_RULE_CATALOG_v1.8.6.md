# Validation Rule Catalog — v1.8.6

- First assignment must bootstrap the current actor as portfolio Administrator.
- Later assignments require an active Administrator in the applicable scope.
- Author, Reviewer, and Approver assignments cannot overlap for one person in overlapping scope.
- Author assignment is required for submission. Reviewer assignment is required for disposition. Approver assignment is required for approval.
- Submitter cannot review; submitter or reviewer cannot approve the same submission.
- Content fingerprint must match at disposition and approval time.
- Ready for approval disposition is required before approval.
- Revocation requires active Approver or Administrator authority and a reason.
- Approval records are retained after revocation or supersession.
- No workflow operation creates an assessment or certification conclusion.
