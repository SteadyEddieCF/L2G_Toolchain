# SSP Roadmap after v1.8.4

1. **v1.8.5 — Portfolio/module JSON and Word Review exchange**: bounded package exchange, module review queues, and round-trip reconciliation.
2. **v1.8.6 — Formal review and approval workflow**: role-aware submission, disposition, and approval records without assessment conclusions.
3. **v1.8.7 — Immutable named baselines**: governed snapshots, comparison, and rollback.
4. **v1.8.8 — Consolidated change and decision register**.
5. **v1.8.9 — Configurable inheritance/reconciliation policy profiles**.
6. **v1.9.0 — Portfolio reporting and delivery package hardening**.

All future releases remain bounded, offline-first, and independently versioned.
