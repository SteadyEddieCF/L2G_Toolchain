# Known Limitations — v1.8.7

- Names and roles remain locally asserted and are not authenticated user accounts or digital signatures.
- FNV-1a fingerprints provide deterministic local comparison, not cryptographic signing or non-repudiation.
- No external identity provider, notification service, workflow server, custody service, or telemetry is used.
- Baselines are authoring-governance snapshots only and do not create assessment or certification conclusions.
- Consolidated portfolio change/decision reporting and external archival signature adapters remain later bounded releases.
