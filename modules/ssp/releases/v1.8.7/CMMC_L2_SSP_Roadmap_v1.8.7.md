2. **v1.8.9 — Configurable inheritance/reconciliation policy profiles**: governed policy selection without weakening protected identities.
3. **v1.9.0 — Portfolio reporting and delivery package hardening**: delivery manifests, package integrity, and offline handoff hardening.
4. **v1.9.1 — Suggested: portfolio health and maintenance dashboard**: operational authoring indicators only; no assessment or certification conclusions.
5. **v1.9.2 — Suggested: evidence freshness and ownership reminders**: local workflow support without external telemetry.
6. **v1.9.3 — Suggested: module dependency visualization and export**.
7. **v1.9.4 — Suggested: controlled bulk authoring and review operations**.
8. **v1.9.5 — Suggested: long-term compatibility and archival validation pack**.
9. **v1.9.6 — Suggested: archival signature and custody receipt adapters**.
10. **v1.9.7 — Suggested: portfolio lifecycle and retirement workflow**.

Items v1.8.8–v1.9.0 are committed roadmap releases. Later items are suggestions, not commitments. All releases remain bounded, offline-first, independently versioned, and SSP-only.
