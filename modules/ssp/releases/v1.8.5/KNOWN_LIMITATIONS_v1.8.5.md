# Known Limitations — v1.8.5

- Cross-portfolio module merge is intentionally blocked; restore a complete portfolio into Single-System mode instead.
- Conflicting related records are reported but not interactively adjudicated in this release.
- Module Word Review does not edit protected identities, hierarchy, inheritance source, formal approval metadata, requirement statements, or assessment objectives.
- Formal approvals and immutable named baselines remain future bounded releases.
