# SSP Roadmap after v1.8.5

1. **v1.8.6 — Formal review and approval workflow**: role-aware submission, reviewer disposition, approval records, segregation of duties, and revocation without assessment conclusions.
2. **v1.8.7 — Immutable named baselines**: governed snapshots, comparison, rollback, and supersession.
3. **v1.8.8 — Consolidated change and decision register**: portfolio-wide traceability across modules, imports, Word review, impacts, and conflicts.
4. **v1.8.9 — Configurable inheritance/reconciliation policy profiles**: governed policy selection without weakening protected identities.
5. **v1.9.0 — Portfolio reporting and delivery package hardening**: delivery manifests, package integrity, and offline handoff hardening.
6. **v1.9.1 — Suggested: portfolio health and maintenance dashboard**: operational authoring indicators only; no assessment or certification conclusions.
7. **v1.9.2 — Suggested: evidence freshness and ownership reminders**: local workflow support without external telemetry.
8. **v1.9.3 — Suggested: module dependency visualization and export**.
9. **v1.9.4 — Suggested: controlled bulk authoring and review operations**.
10. **v1.9.5 — Suggested: long-term compatibility and archival validation pack**.

Items v1.8.6–v1.9.0 are committed roadmap releases. Items v1.9.1–v1.9.5 are suggestions, not commitments. All releases remain bounded, offline-first, independently versioned, and SSP-only.
