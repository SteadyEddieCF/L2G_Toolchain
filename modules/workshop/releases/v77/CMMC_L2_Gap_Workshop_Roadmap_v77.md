## Continuing boundaries

- Workshop remains authoritative for evidence review, evidence requests, provider/responsibility discussion, provider follow-up, gaps, actions, blockers, and facilitated practice conclusions.
- No automatic Met/Not Met, evidence sufficiency, assessment readiness, risk, compliance, certification, or scoring.
- All runtime behavior remains local/offline with no telemetry or remote runtime dependency.
- Stable package identities change only through separately approved contract releases.
